Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

App Name: INFINITY SOVEREIGN (Enlighten.Mint.Cafe)

Developer: Steven Michael

Contact: Rapid City, South Dakota, USA

1. Information We Collect

INFINITY SOVEREIGN is designed with a Sovereign-First architecture. We collect only what is necessary to operate the app:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name (if you choose to create an account). Guest access is available without any personal information.
  • Progress Data: Workshop completion, XP earned, and Trade Passport progression — stored to maintain your learning journey.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To maintain your learning progress and RPG character data
  • To display your Trade Passport and Hybrid Title achievements
  • To authenticate your account (if registered)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data stays within the INFINITY SOVEREIGN ecosystem.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. All API communications use HTTPS. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

INFINITY SOVEREIGN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses OpenAI's API for AI-powered features (contextual generators, deep dives). No personal user data is sent to third-party AI services — only anonymized prompts.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your stored data at any time through the app
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Use the app as a guest without providing any personal information

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing or the in-app feedback system.
