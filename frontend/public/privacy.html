Privacy Policy

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE · by INFINITY SOVEREIGN · Last updated: February 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE ("the App", "we", "us") is a wellness and spiritual practice platform operated under a Private Sovereign Trust by INFINITY SOVEREIGN. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is required to provide the service:

(a) Account data you give us — email, display name, bcrypt-hashed password.
(b) In-app activity required for gamification — module visits, presence timers, Sparks/Dust balances, quest progress, equipped items.
(c) Device data automatically produced by your browser or the Android WebView — user-agent, approximate region from IP for localisation.

We do not collect contacts, call logs, SMS, precise location, biometric data, health records, or any Special-Category data as defined under GDPR Art. 9.

3. How We Use It

Your data is used solely to (a) authenticate your account, (b) award and display your in-app progress (Sparks, Dust, Gaming Cards, 9×9 Crystalline Lattice resonance), (c) operate the Shareable Resonance Pattern (only when you explicitly press Share), and (d) diagnose errors via aggregated, non-identifying logs.

We never profile you for advertising, never sell your data, and never use it to train external third-party AI models.

4. How It Is Protected

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage. All transport uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Databases run inside a private network with restricted credentials. Data integrity is maintained via the Crystal Seal framework using SHA-256. No deprecated hashing algorithms are used. Random values for the internal economy are generated by Python secrets.

5. Third Parties

We rely on a small, vetted set of processors:

(a) MongoDB Atlas — storage.
(b) OpenAI, Google (Gemini), Anthropic — LLM APIs for AI-generated text and imagery (prompts are transmitted, never your account identity).
(c) SendGrid — transactional email (only when you request it).

We do not integrate Facebook SDK, TikTok SDK, or any advertising SDK. No third-party trackers are embedded in the app.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can request at any time:

(i) a copy of all data we hold about you,
(ii) correction of inaccuracies,
(iii) full account deletion.

To request deletion, email sovereign@enlighten.mint.cafe or open Settings → "Delete my account" in the app. Your account, associated wallet, quest log, and inventory are permanently removed within 14 days; backups purge within 30 days.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where GDPR applies) and we do not knowingly collect their data. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be announced in-app at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
