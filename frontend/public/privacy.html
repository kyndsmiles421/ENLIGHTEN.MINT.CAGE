Privacy Policy

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE • Effective: February 20, 2026 • Last updated: February 20, 2026

Your digital sovereignty is the foundation of this app. The principles below describe exactly what we collect, why, and how you reclaim it. Plain language, no dark patterns.

1. Who we are

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE is operated as a sole‑proprietor wellness instrument by Steven Michael, at 318 National Street #2, Rapid City, South Dakota 57702, USA. All privacy inquiries, access requests, and deletion requests go to kyndsmiles@gmail.com.

2. What we collect — and why

Mandatory (required for the app to function)

  • Account info — your email and a bcrypt-encrypted password so you can sign back in.
  • App activity — Sparks earned, quests completed, Mastery Ledger entries, tier status. This is the fuel of your journey; without it your progress resets every session.

Optional (you choose — the app works without them)

  • User‑generated content — journal entries, avatar images, dream logs, and any notes you explicitly save. Stored only if you save them; deletable any time.
  • Messages — conversations with the Sage AI coach are sent to our secured LLM provider (see §4) so the Sage can respond coherently. Stored only to give you session continuity.
  • Device & crash diagnostics — collected by Google Play Console only when the app crashes, so we can fix it. Never linked to your identity.

What we do not collect

  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Your contacts, calendar, or SMS.
  • Access to your photo library or microphone without explicit, feature-scoped consent.
  • Payment card data. When you support the project via the Gilded Path, Stripe processes the card on their infrastructure; we receive only a receipt token.
  • Advertising IDs. There are no ads in this app.

3. How we store it

  • All traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage — the plaintext is never retained.
  • Backend data lives in a managed MongoDB cluster with access restricted to the operator.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or until you request deletion.

4. Third parties we rely on

  • Google Cloud / Emergent — application hosting and logs.
  • OpenAI, Anthropic, Google (Gemini) — LLM providers that power the Sage AI coach. Your messages transit their API under their data-processing terms and are not used by us to train third-party models.
  • Stripe — payment processing for the Gilded Path (if you choose to support).
  • Google Play Services — install delivery and crash reporting.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. Full stop.

5. Your rights

You may, at any time:

  • Access all data we hold about you — request a copy via email.
  • Correct any inaccuracy — reply to your copy with edits.
  • Delete your account and every record attached to it — purged within 30 days.
  • Port your data to another service — we'll export it as JSON.
  • Withdraw consent for any optional feature — toggle it off in Settings.

Send requests to kyndsmiles@gmail.com with the subject "Sovereign Data Request".

6. Children

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE is intended for users aged 13 and over. If you are under 13, please do not create an account. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

If you are a resident of the European Union, United Kingdom, or California, you retain your statutory rights under the GDPR, UK DPA, and CCPA respectively; the rights listed in §5 are the operational mechanism through which we honor them.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and surface a notice inside the app before the change takes effect. Material changes require your continued use as acceptance, or you can delete your account (§5) before the change lands.
