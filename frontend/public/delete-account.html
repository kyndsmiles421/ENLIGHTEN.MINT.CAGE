Delete Your Account

ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE · by INFINITY SOVEREIGN

This page explains how to permanently delete your ENLIGHTEN.MINT.CAFE account and every piece of data associated with it. Deletion can be initiated without reinstalling the app, in compliance with Google Play policy and GDPR Article 17.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, display name, and authentication credentials (bcrypt-hashed password)
  • All earned Sparks, Dust, Gaming Cards, and Trade Passport progress
  • Quest log, inventory items, and equipped gear
  • Resonance Patterns and 9×9 Crystalline Lattice history
  • Journal entries, mood logs, oracle draws, meditation sessions
  • Creator earnings and payout records (if applicable)
  • Notification subscriptions, Founding Architect invite redemptions

Timeline: Active database records are removed immediately. Backup systems purge your data within 30 days.

Retained after deletion: Nothing personally identifying. We keep only aggregate, non-identifying telemetry (e.g., total sessions per day) required for service reliability.

Option 1 — Delete yourself (fastest)

Visit the interactive deletion page and sign in with your account email and password:

Open the Deletion Console

On that page you will:

  1. Enter your email and password
  2. Type DELETE to confirm
  3. Click Permanently Delete My Account

Deletion is instant. You will see a green "Account Deleted" confirmation on screen.

Option 2 — Delete from inside the app

If you're already signed in on Android / iOS / web:

  1. Tap Settings from the Sovereign Hub
  2. Scroll to the Your Data & Account section
  3. (Optional) Tap Download My Data first to save a JSON backup
  4. Tap Permanently Delete Account & Data
  5. Type DELETE, then confirm

Option 3 — Email request (for users who cannot sign in)

Send an email to sovereign@enlighten.mint.cafe from the address on your account.

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

We verify ownership (by replying to the same address), complete deletion, and send a written confirmation within 7 business days.

Before you go — export your data

You have the right under GDPR Article 20 to take a copy of your data with you. In-app: Settings → Your Data & Account → Download My Data. You receive a complete JSON bundle containing every record we hold about you.
